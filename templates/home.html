Voice Analyzer

A voice analyzer, also known as a speech analyzer or voice recognition system, is a technology that processes and interprets spoken language. It utilizes various algorithms and techniques to analyze characteristics of speech such as pitch, tone, frequency, intensity, and duration of sounds. The primary purpose of a voice analyzer is to understand and interpret spoken language, which can have numerous applications across different fields.

1.Recognizing the language being spoken in the audio input.

2.Identifying and transcribing spoken words or phrases into text.

3.Determining the identity of a speaker based on their voice characteristics.
